Artist Camping Terms and Conditions

13. Artists must wear company identification and vest while in camp.

14. Artists without a welfare fee payment slip are not permitted in camp.

15. Artists must bring membership payment slip, welfare payment slip, test results, identification card, premiere ticket slip, location kits, and script.

16. Artists must arrive according to group schedules.

17. For security, all calls should go through customer care while in camp.

18. No visitors are allowed in camp.

19. Artists must bring location kits to camp.

20. Shooting hours are 5am - 5am.

21. Meals are at 8am, 2pm, and 6pm.

22. No capturing or posting movie scenes on social media; violators will face legal action for piracy.

23. Artists who miss the location bus are at their own risk.
